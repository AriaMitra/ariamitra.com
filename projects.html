Projects

  • Project ALU

    In this project, I created a 4-bit ALU that can do addition, subtraction, negatives, multiplication, AND, OR, and XOR. I manually did it with logic gates, creating a half adder, full adder, four bit adder, four bit subtractor, two's complement, and a ripple carry adder.

  • Project 12HR CLOCK

    In this project, I created a chip that basically acts as a 12 hour clock (that displays minutes and hours) with an AM/PM indicator. This works by incrementing in seconds (which is not an output) and checking when to turn over the minute/hour.

  • Project Games

    In this project, I created a chip where people can either play tic tac toe or rock paper scissors. Tic tac toe is a two person game and rock paper scissors is where one person plays against the machine. I did this by creating a 3-bit LFSR to randomly generate numbers.

  • Project Sound Manipulator

    In this project, I worked with team members from the BWSI Basics of ASICs 2025 cohort to create a chip that acts as a sound manipulator. It can change the octave up or down and incorporate a tremolo. These effects can be layered. It also can create a LED blinking in time with the frequency of the sound.
